## Overview

ezyshield verifies **business identity and bank account details** for a contact person, stores an immutable [**verification**](/objects/verification) fingerprint, and lets you run [**checks**](/objects/check) later to detect whether those details have changed. You can subscribe to [**webhooks**](/next-steps/webhooks) for completion events, use [**test BSB codes**](/testing/test-bank-accounts) in non-production, and run [**ABA file checks**](/guides/aba-checks) to validate batches of credits against your verifications.

The [**API reference**](/api-reference/introduction) (including every endpoint) is generated from our [**OpenAPI document**](/api-reference/api.json).
